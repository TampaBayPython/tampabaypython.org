Contribute¶

Tampa Bay Python is a registered 501(c)3 non-profit organization in the state of Florida.

As we’re just starting to get the group back off the ground, help with any of the following is appreciated:

  • Speakers who want to give a lightning or formal presentations

  • Venues for hosting in-person events

  • Sponsoring operational costs for the organization

If you or your company is interested in helping us, please reach out to the lead organizer Matthew Granda, his email is "{handle}@tampabaypython.org".format(handle="mgranda").
