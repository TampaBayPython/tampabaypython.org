Tampa Bay Python¶

Tampa Bay Python is a community of professions, enthusiasts, students, and companies using or learning Python in the Tampa Bay area.

We hold at least one event per month in-person and perform remote office hours twice a month. Anyone is welcome to our meetups regardless of your skill set or skill level.

Our Code of Conduct clarifies our organization’s missions, values, and principals, linking them with standards of professional conduct.

We use Slack to hold online discussions about organizing events, getting technical help, looking or posting about jobs, or just about anything else you’d like to talk about.
